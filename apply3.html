Overview of Job Description

A busy and growing creative agency in Southampton is seeking a Web Designer / Developer to join our expanding team, to design and develop custom built brochure and ecommerce websites. Continuous on-going training and self-development in website technology and online marketing will also be provided.

Established in 2011 this creative agency is home to a team of multi-talented designers striving to build the best creative agency in the Midlands. The position has been made available through growth and is best suited to candidates looking to make their mark on the world of web and design, as well as becoming a pivotal part of a creative agency's growth.
