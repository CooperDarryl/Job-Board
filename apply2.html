Overview of Job Description

Full-stack developer to work in a fun and exciting creative digital agency based in Southampton. You’ll get the chance to work on a range of interesting projects with the latest and cutting edge technologies. We are interested in mid level developers who can hit the ground running, and relish a challenge.

Based in Southampton, the full stack web developer will be involved in the full product lifecycle, from estimating and planning through to delivery, as well as working directly with clients. As a full stack developer, you will need in-depth knowledge of PHP (Drupal, WordPress, Magento CMSs are used), as well as skills in front end development, ideally with JS libraries/frameworks such as Angular and Node.
